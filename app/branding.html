Type

The design features two open fonts: Numbito and Open Sans. Use Numbito for headlines, and Open Sans for copy

Numbito for headlines

Numbito is used for sub-headlines

Open Sans is used for body copy. Open Sans is a humanist sans serif typeface designed by Steve Matteson, Type Director of Ascender Corp. This version contains the complete 897 character set, which includes the standard ISO Latin 1, Latin CE, Greek and Cyrillic character sets. Open Sans was designed with an upright stress, open forms and a neutral, yet friendly appearance.
